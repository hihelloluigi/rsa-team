Match-reaction sound effects, sourced from [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/).

non-commercial use, no attribution required. Embedding in this site is permitted; redistributing the

To swap a sound, just replace the file (keep the same name). `cheer.mp3` is faded out after ~5s in
`src/lib/sounds.ts`, so length doesn't matter.
